Pizza Dough

Cost: $1.11

Ingredients:

3 1/2 cups bread flour, plus more for rolling

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp instant dry yeast

2 tsp salt

1 1/2 cups water at 110‚Ñâ

2 tbsp olive oil, plus 2 tsp

Instructions:

Combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast and kosher salt in a bowl and combine.

Stir in the water and 2 tablespoons of oil and mix until the dough forms into a ball. If the dough is sticky, add additional flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, until the dough forms a solid ball. If the dough is too dry, add additional water.

Scrape the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead into a smooth, firm ball.

Grease a large bowl with the remaining oil, add the dough, and cover with plastic wrap and place in a warm area to double in size, about 1 hour.

Turn the dough out ont a lightly floured surface and divide it into two equal pieces. Cover each with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and allow to rest for 10 minutes.

The dough is now ready to use as desired.
